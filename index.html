Adapt to change, faster.

Your day changes. Your schedule should too. Don't get stuck with a stale schedule or be forced to update one reminder at a time.

With Sorted, sweep to multi-select in a single stroke. Scroll the Time Ruler to offset your reminders. Press and hold to reorder. Tap the calendar to reschedule. Update your schedule on-the-fly and stay on top of your day.
